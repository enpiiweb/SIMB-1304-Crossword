PATRICK STREET, President and Foundation Member of the Australian Crossword Club.

Patrick has been involved with crosswords for many years. His love affair with cryptics began in the 1960s. He joined Alan Eason’s Crossworder magazine in 1988. He was a Foundation member of the Australian Crossword Club when it started in May 1990 and in February 1992 Patrick was privileged to be appointed by the Executive Committee the ACC President and has continued in that role for more than 20 years. He has contributed several crosswords to Crozworld mainly of an unorthodox nature. He took on the challenge of Adjudication of the Slots 1-5 puzzles for ten years. In June 2000 Patrick was appointed to the important role of editor and publisher of the ACC’s monthly magazine Crozworld and has continued this essential undertaking since then. Patrick also carried out the role of Puzzle Set-up person for many years before passing the baton to the indispensable Ian Williams. The ACC is a thriving Club catering for theunique interests of cruciverbalists throughout Australia. The valued members of the ACC are dedicated solvers and lovers of crosswords and quizzes and it is hoped that Crozworld can continue to provide this essential service to the members of the ACC for many years into thefuture.
