The Crozworld Compendium is an annual publication of The Australian Crossword Club. Volume 1 of The Compendium (published in November 2000) featured 31 crosswords and 3 quizzes, all compiled by members of the ACC. Prizes were awarded for two of the crosswords and one quiz.
