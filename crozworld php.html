CrOZworld

Crozworld is the monthly magazine published by the Australian Crossword Club.

The magazine contains six prize crosswords with a variety of styles: cryptic, General Knowledge, thematic, alphabetical jigsaw, Ximenean and innovative.

The compilers are members of the Club and submit their compositions for publication and assessment by the members.

Members are invited to solve the crosswords, send them to an adjudicator and make comments and provide constructive feedback about the crosswords so that a greater understanding of the cruciverbal art is engendered.

Quizzes are published every month for which a book prize is awarded.

Prizes are $50 x 2 for crosswords 2, 3 and 6, crosswords 1 and 5 have $50 plus book prizes and crossword 4 has a special book prize. The prize for slots 1-5 all correct is $100. Plus there are trophies to be won for those solvers with the best score.

In 2011 other special prizes will be awarded for slot 7 and bonus puzzles.
