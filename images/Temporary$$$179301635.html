The Crozworld Compendium is an annual publication of The Australian Crossword Club.

Volume 1 of The Compendium (published in November 2000) featured 31 crosswords and 3 quizzes, all compiled by members of the ACC.

Prizes were awarded for two of the crosswords and one quiz.

Volume 2 of The Compendium was published in November 2001 and features 35 crosswords of various styles.

The Compendium has a variety of crosswords to suit every taste: cryptic, general knowledge, conventional, innovative and many others.
