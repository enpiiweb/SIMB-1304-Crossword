HEXAPENTAGONAL by Barney Naga

The diagram of 64 pentagons has 16 horizontal hexagons intersected by 9 vertical hexagons. Every hexagon forms a word of FOUR letters when read in one direction or another. The horizontal hexagons are clued in order starting from the top and proceeding left to right in successive rows. The vertical hexagons are clued in similar manner proceeding from the top to bottom also. All clues are cryptic. The boundary 28 letters form 4 x 7-letter words which are the names of 4 well-known authors.
