Crozworld is the monthly magazine published by the Australian Crossword Club. The magazine contains six prize crosswords with a variety of styles: cryptic, General Knowledge, thematic, alphabetical jigsaw, Ximenean and innovative. The compilers are members of the Club and submit their compositions for publication and assessment by the members. Members are invited to solve the crosswords, send them to an adjudicator and make comments and provide positive feedback about the crosswords so that a greater understanding of the cruciverbal art is engendered. Quizzes are published every second month for which prizes are awarded. Prizes are $75 per crossword and Quiz except for Crosswords 1 and 5 which have book prizes.
