The Australian Crossword Club was established in 1990 and is a non-profit organisation which aims to provide enjoyment and stimulation to the devotees of the crossword puzzle.

The Club's monthly magazine Crozworld is filled with prize crosswords of all types together with lively discussion between the members about the compositions. A quiz is featured every month.
