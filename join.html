Join the club!

We'd love to have you as a member! Join a growing and vibrant group of crossword creators and solvers.

As a member you will receive the following benefits:

1. CrOZworld newsletter delivered free each month

2. Eligible for prize draws (for crosswords you solve)

3. Submit your own crosswords for possible publication

4. Become part of an exciting group of puzzlers

The Annual membership fees for the Australian Crossword Club are:

Within Australia: Au$40 per annum until December 2014.

Elsewhere: same fees as 'within Australia' if Crozworld sent by e-mail

Method of payment: Cheque drawn in Australian dollars payable to ACC, please.
