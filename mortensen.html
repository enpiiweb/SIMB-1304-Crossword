dear friend, i found a 4 leaf clover

in my garden bed one day,

and so i was expecting luck

would surely come my way.


sure enough, the smiling face

of david p. came by -

my mail is home delivered -

david is a super guy.


then to my amazement

and very great surprise,

i learned that i was lucky -

i had won a lovely prize.


many thanks to all concerned.

it gave me quite a lift

to put it very mildly,

i was really truly spiffed,


now i have my book of words

it should help me a lot.

now, once again, my thanks to you.

yours sincerely, dot.
